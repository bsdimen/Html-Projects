The Alhambra

The Alhambra

A stunning palace and fortress complex located in Granada. The Alhambra is renowned for its intricate Islamic art, lush gardens, and breathtaking views of the Sierra Nevada mountains. Explore its grand halls, including the Nasrid Palaces and the enchanting Generalife gardens.
